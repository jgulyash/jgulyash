## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Tutorial Hell
**Problem:** Watching endless tutorials without building.
**Solution:** 80% building, 20% learning. After 1 hour of tutorial, apply it immediately.

### ❌ Perfectionism
**Problem:** Spending weeks perfecting one project.
**Solution:** Ship early, iterate later. Done is better than perfect.

### ❌ Scope Creep
**Problem:** Adding features nobody asked for.
**Solution:** Timebox projects to 7 days max. Ship v1, then iterate.

### ❌ Isolation
**Problem:** Building without feedback.
**Solution:** Post weekly LinkedIn updates. Join Discord communities. Ask for feedback.

### ❌ Analysis Paralysis
**Problem:** Researching "best practices" instead of building.
**Solution:** Pick a tool and start. You can always refactor.
